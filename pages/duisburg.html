A Partnership for Our City's Future

Guardians of the Planet is proud to partner with the City of Duisburg to empower our youngest citizens to build a sustainable and innovative home.

A Shared Vision for Duisburg

For a Green and Just City

The City of Duisburg is committed to creating a sustainable, innovative, and fair future for all its residents. The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) provide a framework for our local actions, from protecting our green spaces to ensuring quality education.

Together, we believe that empowering children with knowledge about their own city is the best way to foster a generation of active and responsible citizens. This partnership brings local issues into our global missions, connecting children directly with their community.
