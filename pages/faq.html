General Questions

What is Guardians of the Planet?

Guardians of the Planet is a fun, interactive web platform that teaches children aged 7-12 about the 17 UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) through gamified courses, real-world challenges, and local partnerships.

Is the platform free to use?

Yes! We operate on a freemium model. Basic access, including our introductory courses on 5 SDGs, is completely free. A premium subscription unlocks all 17 SDG courses, special certificates, and advanced features for the whole family.

For Parents & Guardians

Is the platform safe for my child?

Absolutely. Safety is our top priority. The platform is a completely ad-free space. All user-generated content, like photos from challenges, is moderated by our team before it appears publicly to ensure a positive and secure environment.

For Teachers & Schools

Is the content aligned with the school curriculum?

Yes. Our content is designed to align with the principles of Education for Sustainable Development (ESD or BNE in Germany), which is an increasingly important part of national curricula. Our platform serves as a ready-to-use digital tool for teachers to meet these requirements in an engaging way.

How do I register my school for a city-sponsored license?

If your city is a sponsoring partner (like the City of Duisburg), there will be a specific registration portal for schools in your area. Please visit our 'Partners' page to see if your city is listed and follow the instructions there. If not, feel free to contact us to discuss options for your school or district.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to help. Contact us and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
