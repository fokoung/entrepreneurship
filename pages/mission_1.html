Module 1: What Does "No Poverty" Really Mean?

Imagine building a house. You need a strong foundation, right? If the foundation is wobbly, the whole house could fall down. For people, the "foundation" for a good life is having their basic needs met.

"Poverty" is when people's foundation is wobbly‚Äîwhen they don't have enough to get the essential things they need to be safe, healthy, and happy. It's more than just not having money.
