Meet BB: The Bin Wizards!

From Germany, we're on a mission to teach every child the superpower of recycling!

What We Do

Making Recycling a Fun Adventure!

At BB, we believe that learning to care for our planet should be exciting. We visit schools and communities across Germany to teach children how to correctly separate trash. Through fun games and stories, we show them how they can be heroes for the Earth just by putting things in the right bin!

Our goal is to make recycling a simple, everyday habit for everyone, starting with the youngest guardians of our planet.

Meet Our Friendly Bins!

Pipa the Paper Eater

Pipa loves to munch on newspapers, cardboard boxes, and old drawings. When you feed Pipa, you help make new books and notebooks!

Plasto the Bottle Gobbler

Plasto has a big appetite for empty plastic bottles and yogurt cups. Recycling with Plasto helps create cool things like new toys and playground slides!

Orgo the Garden Helper

Orgo loves eating apple cores, banana peels, and leftover veggies. Orgo turns them into healthy soil that helps beautiful flowers and trees grow strong.

Our Story

Born from a love for our beautiful German forests and cities, BB was started by a team of parents and teachers. We wanted to create trash bins that weren't just functional, but also fun and educational for kids. Today, we're proud to help thousands of children across the country become confident recycling experts. Together, we are building a cleaner future, one bin at a time!
